Mission & Vision

magô analytics is a catalyst for connection and change.

Who are we?

magô analytics is a collective of learners that aims to practice the ethical use of data while promoting equity and social justice.

Values

Continuous Learning

We are a community of learners who value constant innovation and growth amidst the ever changing world.

Data Ethics

We advocate and uphold the fair, inclusive, and sustainable use of data for society.

Nation-building

We strive to empower and enable organizations with limited data capabilities.
